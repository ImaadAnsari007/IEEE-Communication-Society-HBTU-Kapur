About Us

The IEEE Communications Society at HBTU Kanpur (Geo-Code SBC11519A), is dedicated to fostering innovation in communications technology. We organize workshops, seminars, and events to connect students with industry experts. As a new chapter, we're building a community focused on learning and collaboration.
